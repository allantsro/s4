Bootstrap has its own responsive paddings and margins Wherein its classes, m stands for margins p stands for padding
Bootstrap Padding and Margins can be applied on sides
  • t - sets either margin or padding at the top of our element: mt or pt
  • b - set either margin or padding at the bottom side of our element: mb or pb
  • y - sets either margin or padding on the y-axis, on both the top and bottom: my or py
  • x - sets either margin or padding on the x-axis, on both the left and right: mx or px
  • l - sets either margin or padding on the left side: ml or pl
  • r - sets either margin or padding on the right side: mr or pr
  • blank - sets the margin on all sides: m or p
